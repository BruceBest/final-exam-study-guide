\noindent \textbf{Instructions:} Closed book, 70 minutes. Answer all conceptual questions. \\
\textbf{Name:} \underline{\hspace{8cm}} \textbf{Uniqname:} \underline{\hspace{3cm}}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{[Rasterization / Transformations]}
    \\ Suppose you have a 2D triangle centered at the origin. You want to translate it 5 units right, then rotate it $90^\circ$ counter-clockwise about the origin.
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
        \item Write down the $3 \times 3$ homogeneous matrices $T$ (translation) and $R$ (rotation).
        \item What is the combined matrix $M$? (Hint: order matters.)
        \item Apply $M$ to the vertex $(0, 1)$. What are its final coordinates?
    \end{enumerate}
    \vspace{1cm}

    \item \textbf{[Ray Tracing]}
    \\ When a ray hits a shiny glass surface, what secondary rays are spawned? Briefly explain the physical effect each one simulates.
    \vspace{3cm}

    \item \textbf{[Neural Fields / NeRF]}
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
        \item Why do standard MLPs struggle to learn high-frequency details? (Name the phenomenon.)
        \item How does \textit{Positional Encoding} help?
    \end{enumerate}
    \vspace{2cm}

    \item \textbf{[Geometry / Representations]}
    \\ Compare a \textbf{Triangular Mesh} and an \textbf{Implicit Surface (e.g., SDF)}:
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
        \item Which makes Boolean union (merging two shapes) easier? Why?
        \item Which is better for fast rasterization? Why?
        \item Describe how \textit{Marching Cubes} converts an implicit surface into a mesh.
    \end{enumerate}
    \vspace{2cm}

    \item \textbf{[Reconstruction]}
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
        \item Write the Epipolar Constraint.
        \item Given a point $\mathbf{x}$ in Image~1, what does it constrain in Image~2?
        \item Write the Camera Projection equation (3D world $\mathbf{X} \rightarrow$ 2D pixel $\mathbf{x}$) and label the Intrinsic and Extrinsic parameters.
    \end{enumerate}
    \vspace{2cm}

    \item \textbf{[Generative Models]}
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
        \item One advantage of GANs over VAEs for image generation.
        \item What does the neural network learn during the Diffusion \textit{Reverse Process}?
        \item Purpose of Classifier-Free Guidance and the formula.
        \item How do Flow Models differ from Diffusion in generating samples?
    \end{enumerate}
    \vspace{2cm}
\end{enumerate}
