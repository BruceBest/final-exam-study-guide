\noindent \textbf{Instructions:} Closed book, no notes. Conceptual answers only.

\section*{Question 1: Rasterization}
\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
   \item Why do we use homogeneous coordinates for transformations in computer graphics? Provide one specific mathematical reason.\\
   \vspace{1.5cm}
   
   \item When combining a rotation and a translation, explain why the order matters. Give a concrete example where $T \cdot R \neq R \cdot T$.\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item How does the Z-buffer (Depth Buffer) resolve the visibility problem? What values does it store and update?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item Describe the difference between Bilinear filtering and Anisotropic filtering. In which viewing scenario is Anisotropic filtering most necessary?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Question 2: Ray Tracing}
\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
   \item Write down the Rendering Equation and explain the physical meaning of the integral term $\int_\Omega \dots d\omega_i$. Why is Monte Carlo sampling used to solve it?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
   
   \item Explain the concept of Recursive Ray Tracing. What types of secondary rays are spawned at a surface intersection point?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item Why is a Bounding Volume Hierarchy (BVH) faster than checking every triangle for ray intersection? Describe the traversal logic.\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item Define Radiance and Irradiance. What is the key difference in how they are defined relative to direction?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Question 3: Neural Fields}
\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
   \item List two advantages of Neural Implicit Representations (e.g., NeRF) compared to traditional discrete representations like Voxels or Point Clouds.\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
   
   \item What is Spectral Bias in MLPs? How does Positional Encoding help overcome this problem in NeRF?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item In NeRF volume rendering, why do we predict density $\sigma$ instead of opacity $\alpha$? How are they related mathematically?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item Explain how Importance Sampling is implemented in the original NeRF paper (Coarse vs Fine network).\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Question 4: Geometry}
\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
   \item Contrast Implicit Surfaces (e.g., SDFs) with Parametric Surfaces (e.g., Bézier/Meshes). Which representation is better for checking if a point is inside/outside, and why?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
   
   \item Describe the properties of Bézier Curves regarding control points. Does the curve always pass through all control points?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item Explain how Marching Cubes converts an implicit surface to a mesh. What condition triggers triangle generation in a grid cell?\\
   \vspace{3cm}
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Question 5: Reconstruction}
\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
   \item Write the Camera Projection equation mapping a 3D point $X$ to a 2D pixel $x$. Identify which parameters are Intrinsic and which are Extrinsic.\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
   
   \item Explain the Epipolar Constraint. How does it simplify the correspondence search between two images?\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}

   \item What are the main limitations of Multi-View Stereo (MVS) algorithms? (Give two specific scenarios where MVS fails or struggles).\\
   \vspace{2.5cm}
\end{enumerate}
